Start at IBM

Welcome to Start at IBM!

Congratulations! Start at IBM is the onboarding program with a digital focus and an emphasis on equipping you with the resources you need to be an essential IBMer, prior to your start date.

You will be invited – New Hire Lounge

Once you get your invite, join your orientation cohort slack channel! Attention to the e-mail with subject: .... "has invited you to join a Slack workspace"

This Lounge is designed as a virtual space to keep you connected and will allow you to interact with your Onboarding Success Partner and other cohort members.

How do I login to Slack?

  1. Go to your personal email, open the email from Slack
  2. Click join now and follow the prompts regarding your name and password
  3. Under What I Do? write New IBMer
  4. Agree to terms
  5. Watch tutorial
  6. Click the channel you were invited on the left-hand side, ex: #April_NewYork_NewHires

Or watch this short instructional video

Note: This is an open forum; please do not post or share any kind of personal information or data related to your job offer. It is important to be aware that once you have accepted the New Hire Lounge invitation your personal e-mail will be seen by other pre-hires.
